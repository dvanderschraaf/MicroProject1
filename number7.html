Each circle when pressed, grows in size. The yellow (yellow resembles dishonesty in chinese culture) circle grows more exponentially than the periwinkle color (my favorite color as well as a softer color to resemble honesty). Both circles start with equal size but increase in different amounts per click. I added a restart button so that I could play around with it, I also played around with a function that makes each circle grow with a single click but it made the whole visual concept more difficult.
